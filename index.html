ABOUT

Technology + Art = Me

Animation and interactive media have had their spell over me since I was a small being, and from as early as I can remember my PS2 and crayons were my prized possessions. At the age of seven, I wrote a comic about my toy zebra titled ‘Stripes, The K9 Crusader’. Now I am older and somewhat wiser, I have learnt that ‘K9’ doesn’t quite mean ‘zebra’, and that combining technology and art is a career that will keep inspiring passion in me.

Jade


Freshly graduated from the Bachelor of Design (Communication) at Otago Polytechnic with a specialty in Game Design, I have built on skills in areas such as 3D modelling, world building, animation, and graphic design. Something very important to me is making sure I give back to people where I can; during my studies I was part of the OPSA board and have volunteered in community organizations like Cat Rescue Dunedin and with events such as the South Dunedin Street Festival. Outside of all of that, when I do have the occasional spare moment I ‘research’ games and keep up to date with new technology, though I do occasionally put the devices down, untether myself from the internet, and crochet, paint or make time for friends and family. I am searching for creative work that brings me passion and helps to light that spark in others, so please do get in contact!
